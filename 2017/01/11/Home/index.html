Welcome to my webpage!

Posted on 2017-01-11

My name is Haiqiang Niu and currently I am a postdoctoral researcher at Noise Lab, Scripps Institution of Oceanography, University of California, San Diego.

My research interest includes underwater source localization, geoacoustic inversion and ocean acoustical tomography. Currently, I am working on the ocean acoustic applications based on machine learning (Neural networks).
